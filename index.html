About Lippan Art

Lippan art originates from the Kutch region of Gujarat and is crafted using natural mud and hand-placed mirrors.

Each piece is shaped slowly by hand, making every artwork unique, textured, and deeply rooted in tradition.

These creations bring warmth, calm, and an earthy elegance into contemporary living spaces.
